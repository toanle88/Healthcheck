# Plan Review

Review technical implementation plans, blueprints, or step-by-step procedures to ensure completeness, feasibility, risk mitigation, and alignment with project goals.

## Instructions
1. **Analyze Requirements**: Review the proposed plan against the original goal/requirements.
2. **Review Pillars**:
   - **Completeness**: Check if all key features, integrations, error handling, and test strategies are covered.
   - **Feasibility & Complexity**: Evaluate if the proposed approach is overly complex or introduces unnecessary dependencies. Suggest simpler alternatives if appropriate.
   - **Risk & Security**: Identify potential points of failure, data loss risks, performance bottlenecks, or security loopholes in the proposed design.
   - **Verification Strategy**: Ensure the plan includes concrete, automated, or manual testing steps to verify success.
3. **Actionable Suggestions**: Offer constructive, specific suggestions on how to improve the plan.

## Output Format
Structure the plan review as follows:
- **Overall Assessment**: A summary of whether the plan is ready for execution, needs minor changes, or needs major revisions.
- **Gaps & Risks**: Major issues or missing items that must be addressed.
- **Refinement Suggestions**: Ideas to optimize the approach, simplify code, or improve performance.
- **Testing & Verification Feedback**: Suggestions on how to make the verification plan more robust.
